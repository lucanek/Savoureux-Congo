Welcome to Savoureux Congo

Nestled in the heart of Atlanta, Savoureux Congo invites you to embark on a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of Congo. Our restaurant offers an authentic dining experience that celebrates the rich heritage and diverse culinary traditions of Congolese cuisine.

At Savoureux Congo, we are passionate about bringing the warmth and hospitality of Congo to your table. Our menu is carefully crafted to showcase the traditional dishes that have been passed down through generations, while also incorporating modern twists and influences to delight your taste buds.

From savory stews and grilled meats to flavorful sides and delectable desserts, each dish at Savoureux Congo is prepared with care and attention to detail, using the finest ingredients and authentic recipes. Whether you're craving the bold flavors of Poulet Moambe or the comforting simplicity of Madesu, our menu offers something to satisfy every palate.

Beyond our delicious food, Savoureux Congo is also a place where friends and families come together to create lasting memories. Our warm and inviting atmosphere, paired with attentive service, ensures that every dining experience with us is memorable and enjoyable.

Whether you're a seasoned food enthusiast or new to Congolese cuisine, we invite you to join us at Savoureux Congo and savor the flavors of Congo, where tradition meets taste.

Brazzaville Breeze

Escape to the tropical shores of Brazzaville with our refreshing cocktail. White rum, creamy coconut, tangy pineapple, and zesty lime come together in a delightful concoction that will transport you to paradise. Garnished with a pineapple slice and fresh mint.

Bakongo Sunset Spritz

Experience the vibrant colors and flavors of the Bakongo culture with our Weekly Featured Drink, the Bakongo Sunset Spritz. This refreshing libation is a celebration of tradition and community, featuring a blend of local ingredients and exotic flavors. Crisp and effervescent, it combines sparkling wine with a splash of tangy tamarind syrup, fragrant hibiscus, and a hint of citrus. Served over ice and garnished with a slice of fresh citrus and a sprig of mint, it's a delightful way to toast to the spirit of the Bakongo people.

Bissap (Hibiscus Tea)

Transport yourself to the vibrant markets of the Congo with our Bissap, a traditional hibiscus tea bursting with flavor and color. Brewed from dried hibiscus petals and infused with a touch of sweetness, this refreshing beverage offers a tart and tangy taste sensation that invigorates the senses. Served over ice and garnished with a slice of lemon or a sprig of mint, it's a refreshing and revitalizing treat perfect for any time of day.

Tangawisi (Ginger Juice)

Embark on a taste adventure with Tangawisi, our signature Congolese ginger juice. Crafted with the finest ginger roots and infused with a hint of spice, this refreshing beverage offers a bold and invigorating flavor that tantalizes the taste buds. Served over ice and garnished with a slice of lime, it's the perfect thirst-quencher for any occasion.

Goma Glow

Savor the vibrant allure of our Goma Glow Mocktail. Crafted with guava juice, lemon-lime soda, and a splash of grenadine, each sip is a journey to tropical paradise. Refreshing and visually captivating, it's a must-try on our menu.
